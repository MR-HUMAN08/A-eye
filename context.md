## The Two Problem Statements Considered

### PS 2 — Intelligent Real-Time Blood Logistics Agent
**Domain:** Healthcare / Emergency Services

**Summary:** An AI-powered logistics platform to solve critical delays in blood donor identification and hospital-bank communication during emergencies.

**Minimum Required Features:**
- **Match** — Identify suitable donors in real-time (blood group, geolocation, availability)
- **Alert** — Multi-channel instant notifications to donors, hospitals, blood banks
- **Predict** — Analyze historical behavior to forecast donor response & arrival times
- **Optimize** — Balance blood allocation across facilities to prevent shortages

**Pros:**
- Emotionally compelling, life-saving use case
- Clear real-world impact

**Cons:**
- Requires real GPS infrastructure, donor databases, hospital APIs
- Healthcare/NGO domain — far from Kavion.ai's enterprise focus
- Harder to build convincingly in 30 hours
- Less relevant to the judge's background and hiring interests

---

### PS 7 — FinPath: AI-Powered Behavioral Wealth Architect ✅ CHOSEN
**Domain:** Personal Finance / Enterprise AI / Business Automation

**Vision:** An autonomous "Personal CFO" that bridges the gap between daily spending and generational wealth building.

**The Problem It Solves:**
Most people budget but don't build wealth. There's a disconnect between tracking daily expenses and building a diversified investment portfolio aligned to life goals.

**Multi-Agent Architecture (Minimum Requirements):**

| Agent | Role |
|---|---|
| **Behavioral Analysis Agent** | Uses NLP to categorize spending and identify "Leakage Patterns" (unused subscriptions, emotional spending) |
| **Goal-Modeling Agent** | Uses Goal-Based Investing logic — calculates exact daily savings needed for a target (e.g., house down payment in 5 years), factoring in inflation & market returns |
| **Intelligent Portfolio Agent** | Acts as a Robo-Advisor — suggests asset allocation (Equity, Debt, Gold) based on risk appetite and macro-economic climate |
| **Nudge & Alert Agent** | Provides real-time decision support (e.g., "If you buy this now, your house goal is delayed by 2 months. Proceed?") |

**Pros:**
- Directly aligns with Kavion.ai's DNA (AI + business decisions + automation)
- Rich multi-agent architecture — shows depth of thinking
- The **Nudge Agent** is a jaw-dropping live demo moment
- Buildable in 30 hours with Claude API (no complex external infrastructure needed)
- Universally relatable — every judge understands personal finance
- Clear path to B2C SaaS or enterprise employee wellness product

**Cons:**
- Needs thoughtful UI to feel premium
- Financial data will be mocked/simulated for the demo

---

## Final Decision

> **✅ Build PS 7 — FinPath**

### Reasoning
1. **Judge alignment** — Kavion.ai is an enterprise AI + business automation company. FinPath speaks directly to what the founder builds and funds.
2. **Multi-agent architecture** — Mirrors Kavion.ai's own product philosophy of specialized AI agents working together.
3. **30-hour feasibility** — Can be built cleanly with Claude API + a React/web frontend. No dependency on real-world infrastructure.
4. **Hire signal** — Demonstrates the participant thinks like an enterprise AI product builder, not just a hackathon coder.
5. **Demo wow factor** — The real-time nudge ("this purchase delays your goal by 2 months") is memorable and sticky.
